Product timeline

From crawler to category.

2024 · Q1

Crawler launch

First scrape pipeline goes live. 1,000 listings/day from a hand-picked NJ dealer roster, normalized into a single schema.

2024 · Q3

Dealer roster

491 New Jersey dealers indexed. CMS detection adds Dealer.com, Dealer Inspire, and DealerOn parsers for structured inventory.

2025 · Q1

Real-time updates

Sub-minute revalidation per dealer. Token-cached LLM normalization keeps cost flat as volume grows past 25k listings.

2025 · Q4

Natural-language search

Chat-API ships. "2020+ Camry under $25k near me" plans the query, streams results, and grounds every answer in live inventory.

2026 · Q2

National rollout

Geographic expansion beyond NJ. Pilot launches in PA, NY, CT; dealer SLA + monitoring framework moves to multi-region.
